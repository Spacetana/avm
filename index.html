What is AVM ?

Aclax Virtual Machine is a project made by Akatsuki \ / Needles. It consists of providing a vm service at prices that are accessible to all.

What is the payment method ?

For now, payments will be made only via paypal. Refunds are available for 48 hours.

How to receive my vm ?

You will receive your vm at the email that will be used for payment, it can take from 5 minutes to 15 minutes (depending on the number of orders).
