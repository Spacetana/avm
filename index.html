What is AVM ?

Aclax Virtual Machine is a project made by Atsuki \/ Needles. It consists of providing a vm service at prices that are accessible to all.

How to contact us ?

You can contact us via this email address: aclax@protonmail.com, or find us on discord (click here)

What is the payment method ?

For now, payments will be made only via paypal. Refunds are available for 48 hours.

How to receive my vm ?

You must go to our discord and enter the command e!help to have the command allowing the addition in the queue in order to receive your vm, it can take from 5 minutes to 30 minutes (depending on the number of orders).
